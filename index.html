Short Bio

I am a Bachelor’s student in Software Engineering at Beijing Information Science and Technology University (BISTU), graduating in June 2025. With a GPA of 90.99/100 (ranked 2/91), my research focuses on computer vision, including person re-identification, model lightweighting, and multimodal learning, with expertise in architectures like Vision Transformers and CNNs.

I interned at Tsinghua University’s Intelligent Microsystems Laboratory, where I proposed novel tasks such as Occluded Cloth-changing Person ReID and Audio-visual Integration Person ReID, and conducted research exchanges at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology and the University of Missouri.

I have authored/co-authored 10+ papers in conferences and journals (e.g., ICASSP, IJCNN, Neurocomputing) and received awards such as the National Scholarship and Chancellor’s Scholarship.

Now, I am actively seeking Ph.D. opportunities to advance my work in robust vision systems and embodied AI.
